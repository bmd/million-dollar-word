How to Play:

The goal of the game is to find the "Million Dollar Word" - a word whose letter values multiply out to exactly $1,000,000.

Each letter of the alphabet is assigned a dollar value: A=1, B=2 ... Z=26 (uppercase and lowercase count the same), and a word's overall score is calculated by multiplying its letters together. For example the word "CAT" would have a score of ($3 * $1 * $20) = $60.

It's harder than it sounds. Good luck!
